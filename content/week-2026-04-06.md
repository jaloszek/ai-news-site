### Anthropic

- 🔥 [Claude Managed Agents — hosted long-horizon agent infrastructure (public beta)](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/managed-agents) — Anthropic launched the public beta of Claude Managed Agents, a hosted service that runs long-running agents on your behalf. It decouples the "brain" (Claude + harness loop) from the "hands" (sandbox/execution environment), cutting time-to-first-token by ~60% at p50 and 90%+ at p95. Built-in: checkpointing, scoped permissions, VPC connectivity, credential vaulting that never exposes secrets to Claude-generated code, and persistent sessions across context windows.
  - Positioned as direct competition to n8n and Zapier — developers define agent tasks, tools, and guardrails while Anthropic handles orchestration, context, and error recovery. Enterprises can connect Claude to resources in their own VPCs without network peering.
  - Also: [The New Stack](https://thenewstack.io/with-claude-managed-agents-anthropic-wants-to-run-your-ai-agents-for-you/), [Testing Catalog](https://www.testingcatalog.com/anthropic-launches-claude-managed-agents-for-businesses/)

- 🔥 [Anthropic restricts third-party harnesses (OpenClaw, Paperclip) from subscription access](https://x.com/trq212/status/2009689811616182404) — Effective April 4, Anthropic began cutting off third-party Claude wrappers that bypass subscription rate limits, including OpenClaw and Paperclip. The move drew backlash from users who relied on these tools for higher throughput.
  - Developers depending on unofficial harnesses for capacity management should migrate to the official API or supported SDK integrations.
