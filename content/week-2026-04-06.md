### AI World

- 🟢 [OpenAI releases gpt-oss-120b and gpt-oss-20b open-weight reasoning models](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/9624314-model-release-notes) — Two open-weight models for self-hosted or fine-tuned deployments: gpt-oss-120b for high-throughput production and gpt-oss-20b as a compact alternative. Both are reasoning-class models, available through major hosting providers. A new $100/month Pro plan also launched with unlimited GPT-5.4 and 10× Codex usage vs Plus.
  - GPT-5.3 Instant Mini replaces GPT-5 Instant Mini as the ChatGPT rate-limit fallback, with stronger writing and contextual awareness
  - Also: [GPT-5.3 and GPT-5.4 release notes](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11909943-gpt-53-and-gpt-54-in-chatgpt)

- 🔵 [Google launches Gemma 4, Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite Preview, and Veo 3.1 Lite](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/changelog) — Gemma 4 (26B MoE and 31B dense instruction-tuned variants) drops on AI Studio and the Gemini API under permissive licensing. Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite Preview is the first Flash-Lite model in the Gemini 3 series — designed for cost-efficient inference at scale. Veo 3.1 Lite arrives on Vertex AI as the most cost-efficient Veo video-gen model.
  - Computer Use tool support added to gemini-3-pro-preview and gemini-3-flash-preview, putting browser/desktop control on parity with Claude and GPT-5
