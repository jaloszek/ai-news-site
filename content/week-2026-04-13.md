# AI News: Week of April 13, 2026

## Monday, April 13

### Claude Code

- 🔥 **Model/API** — [AISI Publishes Independent Evaluation of Claude Mythos Preview](https://www.aisi.gov.uk/blog/our-evaluation-of-claude-mythos-previews-cyber-capabilities) — The UK's AI Safety Institute released its formal assessment of Anthropic's restricted-access Mythos Preview model (announced April 7). It finds Mythos solves **73% of expert-level CTF challenges** — the first model to achieve this — and is the first to complete a 32-step real-world network attack simulation end-to-end. Relevant context for AI safety practitioners and enterprise security teams evaluating AI risk posture.
  - Mythos solved "The Last Ones" (a 32-step attack estimated at 20 hours of human expert work) in 3 out of 10 attempts, averaging 22 of 32 steps across all runs
  - AISI cautions that test environments lacked active monitoring and incident response — real-world well-defended systems may not be as vulnerable; recommends prioritising security fundamentals (patching, access controls, logging)

_No new Claude Code CLI releases today (v2.1.101 remains the latest, first seen April 11). For Managed Agents and ant CLI launches from April 8, see the April 6 week file._

### AI World

- 📰 **Industry** — [Wall Street Banks and Governments Begin Mythos Testing](https://www.siliconrepublic.com/enterprise/anthropic-claude-mythos-preview-us-canada-uk-banks-cybersecurity) — JP Morgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, Citigroup, Bank of America, and Morgan Stanley are actively evaluating Claude Mythos Preview as part of Project Glasswing. UK regulators — NCSC, Bank of England, FCA, and HM Treasury — are in discussions about vulnerabilities the model has exposed in their IT infrastructure.
  - US Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent and Fed Chair Jerome Powell formally warned Wall Street CEOs to "take Mythos seriously"; Canadian banks and the Toronto Stock Exchange parent company convened April 10 to assess risk
  - Also: [CNBC coverage of Powell/Bessent briefing](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/powell-bessent-us-bank-ceos-anthropic-mythos-ai-cyber.html), [SecurityWeek analysis](https://www.securityweek.com/anthropic-unveils-claude-mythos-a-cybersecurity-breakthrough-that-could-also-supercharge-attacks/), [InfoQ deep-dive](https://www.infoq.com/news/2026/04/anthropic-claude-mythos/)

- 📰 **Industry** — [Mainstream Press: Anthropic's Model "Too Dangerous to Release Publicly"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PLg2EUkIC78) — ABC News (Australia), CBS Mornings, Fox News Clips, and LiveNOW from FOX all ran segments April 13 on Anthropic withholding Mythos from public release. The story has crossed from tech press into general news, signalling a new phase of public AI-risk discourse. CBS framed it as Anthropic issuing "a stark warning."
  - Mythos was released only to 11 Project Glasswing partners (AWS, Apple, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorgan Chase, Linux Foundation, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Palo Alto Networks) with Anthropic committing $100M in usage credits
  - Also: [red.anthropic.com original announcement](https://red.anthropic.com/2026/mythos-preview/), [Anthropic Project Glasswing page](https://www.anthropic.com/glasswing)
